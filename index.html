About Mayombo Wood and Furinture (MWF) Ltd

At Mayondo Wood and Furniture Ltd, we believe that wood is more than a material, it's the foundation for comfort, structure, and stories.

Based in Uganda, MWF is a proudly local B2B company specializing in sourcing quality timber, poles, and hardwoods from trusted up-country suppliers, which we distribute to construction firms, furniture outlets, and wholesale partners.

But we don’t stop at raw materials. Our on-site workshop brings ideas to life beds, sofas, cabinets, tables, and office essentials all designed with functionality, durability, and aesthetics in mind. Whether you're a contractor or a furniture dealer, our products are built to elevate every space they touch.

Our Commitment

Quality wood. Thoughtful furniture. Transparent operations. We’re here to support Uganda’s businesses, build lasting partnerships, and craft spaces people feel proud of.
